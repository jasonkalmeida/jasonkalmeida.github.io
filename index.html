ABOUT THE SHOW

Where Tech, Politics, and Absurdity Collide.

Let's face it, you need another podcast. There are simply not enough of them out there. We know that you've been looking for the podcast that makes you smarter in under 15 minutes. While We're On The Subject ("WWWOTS" for short) features interviews with famous entrepeneurs to haughty politcians. And most importantly, WWOTS's features debates between Donald Trump and Star Wars Characters. Yes, you always wanted to know where Luke Skywalker stood on important issues. Hosted by two millennial technologists who have a good sense of humor and a solid education in the Socratic method, WWOTS is short ,sweet, and fun <15 minute portions of subjects of substance. It can all be yours...just click the button.... Do it.

RYAN Bethea, CO-HOST

Ryan is an influence marketing consultant at the intersection of politics, start-ups and Hollywood entertainment. Ryan lead grassroots marketing efforts for AMERICA (Lionsgate) and the Dennis Prager Show.

AARON GINN, CO-HOST

Aaron is an experienced Silicon Valley technologist and co-founder of Lincoln. He is one of the original thought leaders of the growth hacking and conservatarian movements. He worked on growth for Everlane, StumbleUpon, and Mitt Romney's 2012 campaign.
